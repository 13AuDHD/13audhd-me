Subscriptions Policy for 13audhd.me

1. No Subscriptions… Yet

As of right now, I don’t offer subscriptions. But someday I might. If and when that happens, here’s what you need to know.

2. Free Content vs. Paid Content

Most of my content is free, and that’s not changing. However, if I ever offer bonus content, exclusive updates, or behind-the-scenes stuff for a price, it’ll be optional. You won’t have to pay to enjoy the basics.

3. Social Media Subscriptions

If I enable subscriptions on platforms like Twitter/X, Instagram, or TikTok, those will be managed by those platforms. That means they handle the billing, not me. If you subscribe there, check their terms for payment, refunds, and cancellations.

4. Email Subscriptions

If I ever start an email list, you can subscribe (or unsubscribe) anytime. No spam, no nonsense... just occasional updates about what’s happening. I won’t sell your email or fill your inbox with junk. Promise.

5. Payment and Cancellations

If I introduce paid subscriptions on this site, here’s the deal:

  • Billing: Payments will be processed through a secure third-party service (like Stripe or PayPal), not by me personally.
  • Cancellations: You can cancel anytime. No hard feelings.
  • Refunds: Depending on the service, refunds might not be available. But if something goes wrong, reach out, and I’ll do my best to help.

6. Changes to This Policy

Since I don’t have subscriptions yet, this policy is basically a placeholder. When (or if) I add subscriptions, I’ll update this page. If you’re still here, you’re agreeing to whatever changes I make.

That’s it! If you ever give me money, I’ll try to make it worth your while. If not, no worries, just enjoy the content :)
